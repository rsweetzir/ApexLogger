What it does

Everything at a glance

🏁

GPS Lap Timing

Set a virtual start line anywhere. ApexLogger auto-triggers on crossing and records split times with millisecond precision.

📡

OBD-II Telemetry

Connect a Bluetooth OBD-II interface to stream real-time boost, throttle, oil temp, coolant, and engine-specific data.

⛽

N54 Fuel Pressure

HPFP and LPFP live monitoring with configurable warning thresholds — critical data for tuned and high-boost builds.

🔧

Per-Cylinder Timing

See timing corrections for all six cylinders in real time. Catch knock before it becomes a problem.

🗺

Walk the Course

Walk your track layout on foot before driving. ApexLogger maps your GPS path and stores it for reference during the session.

⌚

Apple Watch Heart Rate

Arm a workout session from your wrist or phone. Live BPM appears on the gauges page and is recorded with every track point.

📊

Session Review

Browse saved runs with peak speed, elapsed time, and point count. Detailed stats view for every session.

🚗

OBD-Free Mode

No interface? No problem. GPS speed with MPH / KPH toggle is always available — no OBD connection required.
